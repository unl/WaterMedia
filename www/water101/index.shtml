How is water measured?

The unit of measure for standing water in reservoirs and canals is the acre-foot.

Flowing water is measured using cubic feet per second.

What is a cubic foot?

A cubic foot is the volume of a cube of water with each side one foot in length.

What is an acre-foot?

An acre-foot is the volume of one acre of surface area to a depth of one foot. One acre is 66x660 feet, so one acre-foot is exactly 43,560 cubic feet.
